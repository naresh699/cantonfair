<h2 style="font-weight: 800; font-size: 2.2rem; color: #1a1a1a; margin-bottom: 10px;">Step 1: The "Hybrid" Logistics Model</h2>
<div style="height: 10px;"></div>

The biggest killer of Indian dropshipping businesses is **RTO (Return to Origin)**.
If your customer waits 20 days, they **will** refuse the delivery when it arrives.

**The 2026 Solution: The 80/20 Inventory Rule**
*   **Test Phase (0 Inventory):** Run ads for 5 days using Chinese photos. Get 10-15 orders.
*   **Validation Phase:** Buy 20 units from China (via private agent). Ship them to a **Delhi 3PL (Third Party Logistics)**.
*   **Scale Phase:** Ship from Delhi to Mumbai in 3 days. Your RTO drops from 40% to 10%.

> **Pro Tip:** Don't ship direct from China to the customer. Ship China -> Your India Warehouse -> Customer.

<h2 style="font-weight: 800; font-size: 2.2rem; color: #1a1a1a; margin-bottom: 10px;">Step 2: Finding "Private Agents" (Not Suppliers)</h2>
<div style="height: 10px;"></div>

Forget Alibaba for single units. You need a **Sourcing Agent** in Guangzhou or Yiwu.

*   **What they do:** They buy from 1688.com (China's internal cheaper Alibaba), Quality Check (QC) the product, and bundle it to India.
*   **Where to find them:** LinkedIn, Upwork, or Canton Fair.
*   **Cost:** They charge 5-10% commission, but they save you 30% on product costs compared to AliExpress.

<div style="height: 10px;"></div>

Indian buyers are smart. They know what a "fake" site looks like.
To Convert (3%+ Conversion Rate), you need:

*   **WhatsApp Button:** Essential. Indians want to chat before buying.
*   **Indian Phone Number:** +91 Support number in the footer.
*   **Proper Legal Pages:** Terms, Privacy, and Shipping Policy (We have templates for this).
*   **COD Verification:** Use tools like **Pidge** or **GoKwik** to verify addresses via WhatsApp OTP before shipping.

**Q: Is dropshipping legal in India without GST?**
**A:** Technically yes for very small turnovers, but practically no. You cannot get a legitimate Payment Gateway or Facebook Ad Account verification without a GSTIN in 2026.

**Q: How much money do I need to start?**
**A:** In 2026, you need approx **₹25,000**. ₹10k for GST/Legal, ₹5k for Shopify/Platform, and ₹10k for initial Ad testing. Don't start with ₹0.

**Q: Which niche is best for India in 2026?**
**A:** Home Improvement (Tier 2 cities) and Car Accessories. Avoid saturated niches like "Galaxy Projectors" or "Posture Corrector Belts".
